For Business Owners

Your business deserves
an agent who takes it seriously.

Running a business is hard enough. CARVA makes sure the coverage side is handled — with the right policies, the right carriers, and someone who actually understands what you've built.

General Liability

Protects your business from third-party claims for property damage, bodily injury, and more. The foundation of every solid business policy.

Business Owner's Policy

A BOP bundles general liability and commercial property into one smart, cost-effective package — ideal for small and mid-size businesses.

Workers' Compensation

Protects your employees and your business when someone gets hurt on the job. Required in most states — and we make sure you're fully compliant.

Commercial Auto

If your business uses vehicles — yours or your employees' — a personal auto policy won't cover it. We make sure your fleet is properly protected.

Professional Liability

Also known as E&O insurance. Essential for service-based businesses, consultants, and anyone whose advice or work could be questioned.

Commercial Umbrella

When the unexpected is bigger than your base policy, umbrella coverage steps in. A smart, affordable layer of extra protection for your business assets.

We've seen what happens when business owners are underinsured.

CARVA's job is to make sure that never happens to you. We review your coverage every year and adjust as your business grows.
