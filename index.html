For Business Owners

Your business deserves
an agent who takes it seriously.

Running a business is hard enough. CARVA makes sure the coverage side is handled — with the right policies, the right carriers, and someone who actually understands what you've built.

General Liability

Protects your business from third-party claims for property damage, bodily injury, and more. The foundation of every solid business policy.

Business Owner's Policy

A BOP bundles general liability and commercial property into one smart, cost-effective package — ideal for small and mid-size businesses.

Workers' Compensation

Protects your employees and your business when someone gets hurt on the job. Required in most states — and we make sure you're fully compliant.

Commercial Auto

If your business uses vehicles — yours or your employees' — a personal auto policy won't cover it. We make sure your fleet is properly protected.

Professional Liability

Also known as E&O insurance. Essential for service-based businesses, consultants, and anyone whose advice or work could be questioned.

Commercial Umbrella

When the unexpected is bigger than your base policy, umbrella coverage steps in. A smart, affordable layer of extra protection for your business assets.

Cyber Liability

Data breaches don't only hit large corporations. If you store customer data — names, emails, payment info — you could be liable. Cyber coverage protects your business from the financial fallout of a breach or ransomware attack.

Contractors Insurance

Built for contractors, tradespeople, and construction businesses. Includes general liability, tools & equipment coverage, and builders risk — so a single job-site incident doesn't take down your whole operation.

Landlord / Rental Property

Your homeowner's policy doesn't cover a property you rent out. Landlord insurance protects the structure, covers lost rental income if the property becomes uninhabitable, and shields you from tenant liability claims.

We've seen what happens when business owners are underinsured.

CARVA's job is to make sure that never happens to you. We review your coverage every year and adjust as your business grows.

Auto Insurance

We compare rates across top carriers and find the best policy for how you actually drive. Annual reviews to make sure you're never overpaying.

Home Insurance

We walk through every line of your homeowner's policy so you know exactly what's covered — and exactly what's not. No surprises at claim time.

Umbrella Insurance

Extra liability coverage above and beyond your auto and home policies. One of the best values in insurance — and most people don't know they need it.

Life Insurance

The most important — and most personal — conversation in insurance. We have it with care, patience, and zero pressure. Your family's future, protected.

Pet Insurance

Your pets are family too. We help you find the right coverage so unexpected vet bills never force an impossible choice. Plans for dogs, cats, and more.

Renters Insurance

Your landlord's policy covers the building — not your belongings. Renters insurance protects your furniture, electronics, and clothes from theft or fire, and covers you if a guest gets hurt in your home. Often less than $20/month.

Condo Insurance

Your condo association's master policy covers common areas — but not your unit's interior, personal belongings, or liability. CARVA finds a policy that fills those gaps exactly, without paying for what your HOA already covers.

Jewelry & Valuables

Standard homeowner's policies cap jewelry coverage at $1,500–$2,500. An engagement ring, heirloom watch, or fine art collection deserves dedicated protection — we work with Jewelers Mutual and other specialty carriers to cover your valuables at full appraised value.

Classic & Collector Car

Standard auto insurance pays actual cash value — not what your classic is really worth. We work with Hagerty and specialty carriers to insure your vehicle at its agreed value, whether it's a weekend show car or a daily vintage driver.
